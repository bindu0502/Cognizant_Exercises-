Community Bulletin

The annual neighborhood festival returns this weekend with live music, food trucks, and art exhibits. Residents are encouraged to participate in the cleanup drive and visit the pop-up vaccination booth.

Volunteers are needed for crowd management and first aid stations. Sign-up is open on the community website. Stay tuned for updates on the event schedule, guest performers, and safety protocols.

This initiative is supported by the local council and various nonprofit organizations. Let's come together to celebrate and support our vibrant, diverse community!
